About Me

I spent 15 years leading technology delivery at a major bank — managing multi-million programmes, scaling Agile practices across large teams, and bridging the gap between business strategy and technical execution.

Now I'm making the move into product management because I've always been drawn to the why behind the work: what problem are we solving, for whom, and what does success actually look like for the user?

My background gives me something most PMs don't have on day one: a deep understanding of how delivery works, what makes engineers productive, how stakeholders think, and how to navigate large organisations to get things shipped.

Risk Reporting Platform

BMO · Product Owner

Redesigned as a self-serve portal with transparent risk thresholds. Drove adoption from zero to 250+ users. Executive reporting requests dropped 60%.
